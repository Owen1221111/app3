Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2024

Introduction

This is a local-only asset tracking application. We highly value your privacy and want you to understand how we protect your personal information.

Data Collection and Use

This application:

  • Does not collect any personally identifiable information
  • Does not require network access
  • Does not upload your data to any servers
  • All data is stored locally on your device only

Data Storage

All information you enter in the application (including:

  • Asset amounts
  • Cash records
  • Exchange rate settings
  • Personal notes

is stored only in your device's local storage using iOS's UserDefaults system for secure storage.

Data Deletion

You can delete your data at any time by:

  • Deleting individual records directly in the application
  • Removing the application will completely clear all related data

Third-Party Services

Third-Party Services: - This application uses Yahoo Finance API to provide real-time exchange rate information - Requests are sent to Yahoo Finance when querying exchange rates - We do not share any of your personal information with Yahoo Finance - Please refer to Yahoo Finance's Terms of Service and Privacy Policy for more information: https://legal.yahoo.com/us/en/yahoo/privacy/index.html Data Usage: - Yahoo Finance API is only used to obtain real-time exchange rate information - We do not collect or share your personal information - All accounting records are stored locally on your device only

Children's Privacy

This application is not targeted at children under 13, and we do not intentionally collect personal data from children.

Privacy Policy Updates
